Self-Sufficiency

Going off the grid means you’re responsible for every part of your life: energy, water, food, and shelter.

Why People Choose the Off-Grid Lifestyle

Some are seeking freedom from rising utility costs. Others want a closer connection to nature or are preparing for emergencies. Whatever the reason, it’s a growing trend.
